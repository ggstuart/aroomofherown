Independent cultural broker, project planner, bid writer and evaluator

Arts and cultural funding bids can be highly complex and time consuming. I have extensive experience of evaluating and developing cultural & creative projects and organisations; and writing competitive arts & heritage sector bids. I have an excellent success rate in securing Arts Council funding. I have developed and submitted applications for both individuals and organisations.

What I do

I am a highly experienced cultural and creative sector professional with a proven track record in project planning, brokering partnerships and securing resources. I have recently worked with two arts organisations securing them Arts Council National Portfolio Organisation (NPO) funding equating to £1.4million. I have excellent communication and leadership skills particularly in enabling and encouraging organisations and individuals to reach their full potential.

I am a well-respected creative and cultural advocate, delivering strategic arts projects and utilising extensive regional, national and international contacts and networks. Whilst working for Arts Council England I amassed over 10 years’ experience of working with the country’s top arts venues, facilitating and soliciting strategic programmes and assessing and evaluating funded projects. I am an experienced evaluator having evaluated the qualitative and quantitative success of projects undertaken by individual artists and national organisations like UK Young Artists and Design Nation.

I have been an independent consultant for over 8 years. I offer a host of client services to both organisations and creative individuals. I offer expertise and can support you in three stages of a project lifecycle.

  1. Preparation and planning

    Where are you at? Where do you want to be? How can you get there? I can support you to clarify what you want to achieve, what the outcome will be, what you need to do and who you need to influence to meet your aims.

  2. The proposal/how are you going to resource it?

    Once you understand the journey you need to go on and your outcome I can help you prepare your case for support. I can help you identify the most appropriate funding options and guide you through the application processes.

  3. Evaluation

    What are the lessons learned? What are the next steps? I can help you to evaluate your project. My plural & open methodology will provide you with a clear understanding enabling you to continue in your creative and professional development.

I have a proven track record in working with high quality creative projects. I know how to present you, your organisation and projects to significantly strengthen the likelihood of success.

Accessing Arts Councils support for bid writing

The Arts Council want to ensure everyone has an opportunity to apply for a grant. If you anticipate any barriers in making an application and require help, the Arts Council can pay for someone to support you.

This facility has enabled me to write successful bids on artist's behalf. To find out if you qualify, contact customer services.
