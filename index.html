Tina Smith

Independent cultural bid writer and evaluator

Arts and cultural funding bids can be highly complex and time consuming. I have extensive experience of evaluating and developing cultural & creative projects and organisations and writing competitive arts and heritage sector bids. I have an excellent success rate in Arts Council grants for the arts applications I have developed and submitted for both individuals and organisations.

What I do

I am a highly experienced cultural and creative sector professional with a proven track record in project planning, brokering partnerships and securing resources. I have recently worked with two arts organisations securing them Arts Council National Portfolio Organisation (NPO) funding equating to £1.4million. I have excellent communication and leadership skills particularly in enabling and encouraging organisations and individuals to reach their full potential.

I am a well-respected creative and cultural advocate, delivering strategic arts projects and utilising extensive regional, national and international contacts and networks. Whilst working for Arts Council England I amassed over 10 years’ experience of working with the country’s top arts venues, facilitating and soliciting strategic programmes and assessing and evaluating funded projects.

I have been an independent consultant for over 8 years. I offer a host of client services to both organisations and creative individuals. I have built on my proven track record in recognising good quality creative projects to develop and invest in. I know how best to present your organisation and projects to significantly strengthen the likelihood of success.

Accessing Arts Councils support for bid writing

The Arts Council want to ensure everyone has an opportunity to apply for a grant. If you anticipate any barriers in making an application and require help, the Arts Council can pay for someone to support you.

This facility has enabled me to write successful bids on artist's behalf. To find out if you qualify, contact customer services.
