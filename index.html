Tina Smith

Independent cultural broker, mentor and bid writer

Arts and cultural funding bids can be highly complex and time consuming. I have extensive experience of assessing and developing cultural & creative projects and organisations; and writing competitive arts & heritage sector bids. I have an excellent success rate in securing Arts Council funding. I have developed and submitted applications for both individuals and organisations.

What I do

I am a highly experienced cultural and creative sector professional with a proven track record in mentoring, brokering partnerships and securing resources. I have worked with UK Young Artists and Bamboozle Theatre securing them Arts Council National Portfolio Organisation (NPO) funding equating to £1.4million. I have excellent communication and leadership skills particularly in enabling and encouraging organisations and individuals to reach their full potential.

I am a well-respected creative and cultural advocate, delivering strategic arts projects and utilising extensive regional, national and international contacts and networks. Whilst working for Arts Council England I amassed over 15 years’ experience of working with the country’s top arts venues, facilitating and soliciting strategic programmes and assessing projects.

I have been an independent consultant for over 10 years. I offer a host of client services to both organisations and creative individuals. I offer expertise and can support/mentor you in these areas.

  1. Preparation and planning

    Where are you at? Where do you want to be? How can you get there? I can support you to clarify what you want to achieve, what the outcome will be, what you need to do and who you need to influence to meet your aims.

  2. The proposal/how are you going to resource it?

    Once you understand the journey you need to go on and your outcome I can help you prepare your case for support. I can help you identify the most appropriate funding options and guide you through the application processes.

I have a proven track record in working with high quality creative projects. I know how to present you, your organisation and projects to significantly strengthen the likelihood of success. If you prefer to write your application yourself, I offer a "critical friend" reading service where I will look over your application and discuss with you ways you can strengthen and improve it.

Accessing Arts Councils support for bid writing

The Arts Council want to ensure everyone has an opportunity to apply for a grant. If you anticipate any barriers in making an application and require help, the Arts Council can pay for someone to support you.

This facility has enabled me to write successful bids on artist's behalf. To find out if you qualify, contact customer services.
